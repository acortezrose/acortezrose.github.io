  • Good content reigns in audiences and success
    • Good web content pulls the user in by combining what you want to say with what your audience wants to read
    • Not discussed: search engine and SEO echo chambers, impacts of AI?
  • Design for saccades
    • Eyes jump from place to place to take in information, then fill the gaps in between
    • Jumps are called saccades (sah-kade)
    • The more you read, the better you jump
    • Common words, sentence case, and comfortable line lengths to support most people’s saccades
  • The best way to get sign off is to bring the sign off-ers on a journey with you
    • Get people who knows things together to talk and share knowledge so everyone’s on the same page
    • Introduce evidence or facts to generate conversation, "is this your experience?" and other open ended questions
    • Listen for anecdotes and repetition
    • Ends when everyone who needs to agrees on next steps
    • After discoveries, create a mental map that others can see, remember, correct, spot holes in
  • Know and design for your current audience
    • Audiences change over time, stay up to date
    • They want things. Often conveyed thru
      • Popular links
      • Search terms - digital language is not always the same as verbal or conversational language
      • Time on page - what is the expected time based on content form?
      • Unique visits - before removing, ask what might cause low visits
      • Bounce rates - how fast do people leave? is it because theyre getting where they want to go?
    • They use specific language
      • See Google trends, competitors
    • They have a life outside your content
      • Referrals and marketing efforts
    • Accurate audience research carries a different amt of weight with different people/companies
