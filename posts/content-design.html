  • The Science of Reading
    • eyes jump from place to place to take in information, then fill the gaps in between
    • jumps are called saccades (sah-kade)
    • the more you read, the better you jump
    • use common words
    • use comfortable like lengths to support jumps
    • sentence case supports easier reading because people are used to it
  • Content Discovery and Research
    • the best way to get sign off is to bring the sign off-ers on the journey with you
    • what is a content discovery
      • getting people who knows things together to talk
      • sharing knowledge so theyre all on the same page
      • introduce evidence or facts to generate conversation
      • "is this your experience?" and other open ended questions
      • listen for anecdotes and repetition
      • ends when everyone who needs to agrees on next steps
      • After discoveries, create a mental map that others can see, remember, correct, spot holes in
